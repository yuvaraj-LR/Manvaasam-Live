About Bhumii

Bhumii Conservation Centre was created by Gnanasuria Bahavan Devaraj, the Founder Director of the Non-Government Organization, to create awareness on the Conservation of Natural Resources, Environment and addressing the impact of the Climate change particularly for the Tamil World. Bhumii has been working among the rural and agricultural community. Gnanasuria bahavan Devaraj is an expert in Agriculture, Conservation of natural resources and a multimedia expert. He has published more than 400 postings in his Blog and four books in Kindle publication.

Our Founder

D.Gnanasuria Bahavan, a 71 years old, graduated in Agriculture and post-graduated in Journalism and Mass Communication. I am very proud of my parents, my father Mr.R.Devaraj was a village school Headmaster and a versatile man. My mother by name D.Mangalakshimi was an efficient Home Manager. I graduated in Agriculture and post-graduated in Journalism and Mass Communication. I was employed in the Government Machinery, as Agricultural Officer for 6 years and as a Broadcaster for 18 years in All India Radio and served for Agriculture and Rural Development. I took voluntary retirement in the year 1995 and founded my own Service Organization, BHUMII TRUST to work for the rural people for the sustainable development of Natural Resources and Agriculture. Thanks to Google and other online platforms, I have formed Whatsapp groups, Blogspot, and Youtube programs to continue my services for the development of Natural Resources and Agriculture. To my credit, I have authored and published around 800 articles and 150 Video programs to follow my Vision and Mission. I was thrice awarded for my Short Stories, Novel and for one of my stage dramas, one of my short stories was prescribed as the syllabus for postgraduate students of Madurai Kamaraj University.
